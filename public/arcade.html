Bring your own game

Take any simple game and put it onchain: every move a real transaction, one tap, no wallet. Fork the open template (the Sprint stack) or roll your own. Meet the bar below and your game gets a permanent cabinet right here.

01One-tap gasless play. An ephemeral key in the browser, transactions relayed for free. No wallet, no install.
02An onchain score or shared state. A leaderboard or a shared tally the hub can read.
03Uncheatable scores. The score is the count of real transactions a player actually sends, so it can't be faked from the client without sending them onchain.
04The standard actions event. Emit arcadeAction so your game feeds this counter.
Read the build kit Submit your repo, hosted URL, and contract address. Builds pass a safety and quality review (no malicious contracts, works on mobile) before they are featured.
Test the network before mainnet | Games run on MultiversX testnet
