How it works

Red transactions are pending drains on a valueless game token. Tap one to fire a real gasless counter-transaction that claws the amount back before its settle window closes. Green ones are inflows; leave them (recall one by mistake and you forfeit it). You are not reversing a settled transaction; you are countering it fast enough. The round seed and your final balance are computed onchain, so the score can't be faked, and every run is unique.
Experience Supernova before mainnet, on MultiversX testnet. Counter-transactions read straight from the chain.
NOVA is a valueless in-game token. No real funds are ever at stake. A testnet experiment; the board can reset with testnet.
